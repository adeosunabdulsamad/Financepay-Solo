FinancePal

FinancePal is your go-to personal finance budgeting app designed to help you take control of your finances. Whether you're looking to track your daily expenses, set savings goals, or plan for future investments, FinancePal provides the tools you need to manage your money effectively.

Key Features

Expense Tracking

Monitor your daily expenses efficiently and stay on top of your budget.

Bank Integration

Sync your transactions seamlessly with MySQL and the Plaid API.

Custom Reports

Generate personalized financial reports to analyze your spending habits.
